Buchepalli Venkayamma Subbareddy Engineering College was established in the year of 2008 by Visionary leaders Sri. Buchepalli Subba reddy and Smt. Buchepalli Venkayamma, under Sri Surya Social Service Society, with a noble aim of providing high quality technical education to rural area back ground students. BVSR Engineering College has earned a distinction of its own in imparting top-quality engineering education in the Prakasam District region. Since inception the college has been churning out competitive engineering graduates with a global outlook. BVSR Engineering College has its impressive built-up area of 1,70,000 Sq.feet, housed in 12 acres of site, with lush green surroundings; and is located at 19 km away from the Prakasam District Headquarters Ongole, in the medium sized town: Chimakurthy - the icon for Galaxy granite on the Ongole to Kurnool State Highway.
